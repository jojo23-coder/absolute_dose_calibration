Notes Protocol notes
  • `Dw,Q = MQ × ND,w × kQ,Q0`, with `MQ = M × kTP × kelec × kpol × kS`.
  • Default chamber data is seeded from your workbook for `ND,w` and from the 2024 IAEA TRS-398 Rev. 1 kQ table for `IBA FC65-G`.
  • The legacy workbook `kS` lookup is preserved because your spreadsheet depends on it, but the recommended mode is the two-voltage method.
